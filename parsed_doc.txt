#1  
ROLEX — Submariner Date (Ref. 126610LN)
BRAND
Rolex
MODEL
Submariner Date (Ref. 126610LN)
TYPE
Dive Watch / Mechanical (Automatic)
FOUNDED
1905, London (HQ Geneva)
PRICE (USD)
$10,100
OVERVIEW
The Submariner is the quintessential dive watch and arguably the world's most iconic timepiece. Introduced in 1953, this 41mm Oystersteel model features a unidirectional rotating Cerachrom ceramic bezel, 300m water resistance, and the in-house Calibre 3235 movement (70-hour power reserve). Its scratch-resistant black ceramic bezel and Oyster bracelet make it equally at home in the boardroom or the deep sea. The 'Sub' set the standard for the modern dive watch aesthetic that hundreds of brands have tried to emulate.

OVERVIEW
Named after the Daytona International Speedway, the Cosmograph Daytona is Rolex's iconic racing chronograph. The 40mm Oystersteel case houses the Calibre 4130, a COSC-certified chronograph movement. The black Cerachrom ceramic bezel with tachymetric scale and panda-style subdials make it one of the most coveted watches on the planet. Waiting lists at authorized dealers can stretch years. Paul Newman's personal Daytona sold at auction for $17.8 million in 2017.
